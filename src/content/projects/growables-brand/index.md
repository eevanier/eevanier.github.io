## Overview

Growables is a brand focused on making gardening accessible to everyone, regardless of space. The branding reflects this mission with a clean, modern, and inviting aesthetic.

## Brand Elements

- **Logo**: A simple, memorable mark that combines elements of nature and growth.
- **Color Palette**: Inspired by natural earth tones and vibrant greens.
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts for readability and approachability.

## Packaging

Designed eco-friendly packaging that aligns with the brand's sustainability values while standing out on the shelf.
