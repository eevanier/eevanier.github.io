## Overview

The Growables Kit is the flagship product of the Growables brand. It is an all-in-one solution designed to help anyone start their own garden, even in small apartments.

## Design Features

- **Compact Footprint**: Designed to fit on windowsills and small tables.
- **Eco-Friendly Materials**: All components are biodegradable or recyclable.
- **Modular System**: Users can expand their garden by connecting multiple kits together.

## User Experience

Focused heavily on the unboxing and setup process to ensure it is intuitive and rewarding for first-time gardeners.
