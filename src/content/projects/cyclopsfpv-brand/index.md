## Overview

Cyclops FPV is a brand dedicated to the FPV drone lifestyle. The branding project focused on creating a visual identity that is both aggressive and professional, appealing to competitive pilots and hobbyists.

## Design Philosophy

- **Bold & Iconic**: The logo is designed to be easily recognizable even at small sizes or when in motion.
- **Tech-Forward**: The aesthetic draws inspiration from HUD displays and cyberpunk themes.

## Brand Assets

The project delivered a comprehensive brand guide, including logo variations, color codes, and typography usage to ensure consistency across all future media.
